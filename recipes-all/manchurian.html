Vegetable Manchurian

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated cabbage
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2-3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2-3 tablespoons cornflour
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2-3 spring onions, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine grated cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, and onions.
  2. Add all-purpose flour, cornflour, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to form a dough-like consistency.
  3. Shape the mixture into small balls.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the vegetable balls until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  5. In another pan, heat a little oil and saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  6. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, chili sauce, and vinegar. Stir well to combine.
  7. Add the fried vegetable balls to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

Notes

Vegetable Manchurian can be served as an appetizer or as a main course with fried rice or noodles. Adjust the level of spiciness according to your preference.
