Chow Mein

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (any type)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup green beans, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 spring onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced onion, minced garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the carrot, bell pepper, cabbage, and green beans. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  4. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss to combine with the vegetables.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce, chili sauce, and tomato ketchup. Season with salt and black pepper. Toss everything together until well coated.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

Notes

Vegetable Noodles can be customized with your favorite vegetables. You can also add tofu or paneer for extra protein. Adjust the sauces and spices according to your taste.
